# Dubito
Doubt is a card game. It begins with the maximum number of cards allowed by the deck, and the player with the most cards starts. A player can place 1 to 3 face-down cards on the table and state a number associated with them (e.g., 2 8, meaning the player has placed two cards, and they are both 8). However, the player can either be bluffing or telling the truth, and the next player must decide. The cards accumulate until someone doubts. At that point, the last cards played are revealed. If the player was bluffing, the cards go back to them; otherwise, the cards go to the one who doubted. The winner is the one who gets rid of all their cards.
